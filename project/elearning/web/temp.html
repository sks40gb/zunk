	Fees
	Concession 
	The following discounts to fees are available for many courses: 
	50% to (i) those whose only source of income is Social Welfare payment. 

        (ii) those in receipt of old age non-contributory pension. 

	Concessions are also available to: 

	(i) Staff and Alumni Association members of UCD 

	(ii) Registered students of any higher education institution. 

	Please note that discounts can only be allowed on production of appropriate 
	documentary evidence. 

	For those who wish to avail of discounts, contact the adult education office 
	directly at 

	Tel: 706 1695 / 706 1376 / 706 1416 / 706 1573
	Refunds 
	Fees which have been paid are not refunded for any reason except when a
	course does not proceed or is already full.
